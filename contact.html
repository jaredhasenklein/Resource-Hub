What is your policy on ads and cookies?
We use ads to support this site. We don't use cookies, but our providers may. Here's the full policy:
Ads
This site is not free to run. Thanks to the generous support of Github, our Hosting costs are free. Twitter Bootstrap provided us (and the rest of the internet) with this great CSS framework free of charge, too. However, there are other costs like domain registration and online storage. I am a college student who has been making these resources (almost) alone since 9th grade and I put in countless hours because I really enjoy it and really want to help. However, I do not pay myself anything for doing this.

That all being said, I don't want to be out-of-pocket any more than I have to be. So, starting in 2017, I began serving ads on the resources. I hope that they don't get in the way too much! If you have an ad-blocker, I'll still let you use the site, but would appreciate it if you would pause for all websites *.jaredhk.com.

Additionally, if you are so inclined, I would greatly appreciate donations to help defray costs. You can donate via PayPal here. Please note, donations are NOT tax deductible and there is no direct reward for donating.

Cookies
We do not place any cookies directly on the site. However, our ad provider (Google's AdSense) may. Also, most of our resources use 3rd party services/vendors. They each have their own policies and may be tracking via cookies. We have no control over this. Our providers include (but are NOT limited to):
  • Google Ad Sense
  • Google Analytics
  • Google Sheets and Doc
  • Microsoft One Drive
  • YouTube
