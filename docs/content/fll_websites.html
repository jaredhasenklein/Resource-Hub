In addition to my yearly FLL sites, these are some of my favorites.
Note: I may update this list at any time. Check back later for more resources from around the web!
  • Techbrick
    Techbrick provides amazing FLL resources each year and links to other resources around the web. That site was much of my inspiration for starting the Resource Hub. Visit Techbrick
  • EV3 Lessons/FLL Tutorials
    Tutorials for every component of FLL Visit FLL Tutorials Visit EV3 Lessons
  • FLL Share & Learn Facebook group
    A global community of FLL mentors and volunteers. Please note: this group has very strict rules and often restricts the extent that people are able to help each other. Please read the rules as the admins take them very seriously.Visit FLL Share & Learn
  • Robot Mats Family of Products
    Training mats for FLL, brought to you by the folks over at Techbrick. Robot Mats for FLL practice, Creative Playmats for kids, and Empower Mats for visually impaired students in robotics. Disclaimer: Robot Mats has been a sponsor of my websites in the past. However, them appearing on this list is not a result of the sponsorship. They really are one of my favorite FLL sites. Visit Robot Mats Visit Creative Playmats Visit Empower Mats
  • STOW-or-GO Portable Table
    Build a portable table you can fit in your car and bring it to events to practice. Check out these detailed (but dated) instructions. Visit STOW-or-GO instructions
  • Northern California FLL Shop
    The Northern California region of FLL sells used game boards and other valuable components at a discount. Old FSKs can be very expensive so if you are looking for one to buy, this is a good place to start. Visit NorCal FLL Shop
