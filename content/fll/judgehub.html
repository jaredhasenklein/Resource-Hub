Guided notetaking/tiebreakers

These documents will help judges to "check all the boxes" before deliberations and make sure they get all the content they need in one place. This is a fairly exhaustive list of areas but teams will often bring in additional factors beyond those mentioned on the sheet. Remember that as a judge, it is your responsibility to find out as much of these areas as possible for more fair deliberations. NEW: There are several versions below. On each document, page one is to fill in and take notes and page two has coresponding sample questions and guidance. You can see the 7/sheet or 3/sheet versions, depending on your space needs. You may need more than one sheet per event depending on how many teams you will see and how many notes you take. The fillable PDF version will let you type on the computer while the printable PDFs are ideal for handwritten notes. Finally, the Word versions will let you edit them for your needs.
