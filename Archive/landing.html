COVID-19 UPDATE AND INFO ON THE 2020-2021 SEASON

FIRST has announced many updates for the current and upcoming seasons. Importantly, all current season FRC events and any remaining FLL or FTC events have been canceled. They will not be played at a later date. The unified theme for the 2020-2021 season is FIRST Game Changers. The 2020 FRC game (INFINITE RECHARGE) will be replayed.

FLL will likely be virtual in certain regions. Likewise, we've put out a brand new guide on FLL in the virtual era. Check it out today!
